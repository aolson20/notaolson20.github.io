Althau Menhirs

3 Mar 2019

Site in Aargau Switzerland found by Analyssa Olson and Tony Olson using GPS and coordinates.

This is the best site found so far because of the atmosphere on the day of discovery. The fog had just set in and there were remnants of a supposed witch ritual from the night before in the form of a boss and flower offering in the middle of one of the stone circles in the feild.
